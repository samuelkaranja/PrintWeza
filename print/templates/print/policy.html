PRIVACY POLICY

We are committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information. As part of this commitment, we have developed this Privacy Policy, which outlines the principles and guidelines by which Printweza operates to protect the personal information we collect, use, disclose or otherwise manage in the course of our operations.

What personal information does Printweza collect?

Personal information is any information that identifies you, or by which your identity could be deduced, including, without limitation, your name, e- mail address, home address, telephone number, age, marital status, gender, income, date of birth, Mpesa number, and any other information that identifies who you are or would allow someone to contact you.

Limitations on Use, Disclosure and Retention of Personal Information

  1. Personal information will not be used or disclosed for the purposes other than those for which it was collected, except with the consent of the individual or as required by law. Personal Information will be retained only for as long as is required or appropriate to fulfill the identified purpose.
  2. Printweza will record any new purpose for which it has obtained an individual's consent to use, retain or disclose information.
  3. Printweza does not sell, rent or trade personal information. Personal information is only disclosed to third parties who have signed an agreement binding them to Printweza's Privacy Policy.
  4. Printweza will establish criteria for the retention of personal information, including, as appropriate, minimum and maximum retention periods.
  5. Personal information that has been used to make a decision about an individual will be retained for a reasonable period to allow the individual access to the information after the decision has been made.
  6. The documents you have submitted will be open to deletion from our servers by you at any time. No copies of the work will be saved once you delete them.

Consent

  1. The cornerstone to protecting your privacy is consent. We will only collect, use, and disclose your personal information with your consent except where otherwise permitted or required by law. Depending on the type of personal information collected from you, and the purpose for which the information is collected, your consent may be express or implied. Express consent can be granted verbally, in writing, in person, electronically, or by telephone. Your consent is implied if we ask you to provide personal information with a stated purpose.

By using printweza and providing Printweza with your personal information , you agree and consent that we may collect, use and disclose your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you provide us with the personal information of another individual, you represent that you have all necessary authority and/or have obtained all necessary consents from such individual to enable us to collect, use and disclose such personal information for the purposes set forth in this Privacy Policy.

Security Safeguards

Printweza strives to ensure that its security measures will protect personal information against such risks as loss or theft, unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use, modification or destruction, regardless of the format in which the information is held. Such measures will vary depending on the sensitivity, amount, format, nature and storage of the information and will involve, as applicable, physical, administrative, technological, as well as electronic and online security measures. This Privacy Policy may be updated and revised from time to time. All changes to our Privacy Policy will be posted on this website. Whenever we update this Privacy Policy, we will change the date to reflect the last date of revision.
